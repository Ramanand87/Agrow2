Services

Precision

Utilizing ML model and image recognition to monitor crops, soil conditions, and weather patterns. This can involve providing farmers with insights on optimal planting times, irrigation scheduling, and disease detection.

Weather Forecasting and Risk Management

Providing weather forecasting services tailored to the needs of farmers, including alerts for extreme weather events such as storms, frosts, or droughts. Additionally, offering risk management tools such as crop insurance and hedging strategies to mitigate weather-related losses.

Offering services for monitoring crop health, detecting pests and diseases, and providing recommendations for pest control and crop management practices.
